---
title: "CachedCommitments"
description: "Caches the commitments per participant and the commitments per stakeholder group in a period, in order to optimize the computation of commitments for the subsequent period. It optimizes the computation of a counter-participant commitments when at most half of the stakeholder commitments shared with that participant change in the next period."
---
