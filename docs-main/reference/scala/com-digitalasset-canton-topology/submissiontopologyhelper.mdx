---
title: "SubmissionTopologyHelper"
description: "Retrieve the topology snapshot used during submission by the submitter of a confirmation request. This can be used to determine the impact of a topology change between submission and sequencing. An example usage is during validation of a request: if some validation fails due to such a change, the severity of the logs can sometimes be lowered from warning to info."
