## Credential Design

In this tutorial there are two sets of credentials:

- one set for role (Provider, Registrar and Holder) onboarding in the Registry
- one set for token-specific roles (BOND issuer and BOND holder) in the Registry

Here is the summary:

| Entity | Provider | Registrar | Issuer | Investor1 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Roles in Registry | Provider | Registrar | Holder | Holder |
| Credential for role onboarding | [provider] [hasRegistryRole] [Provider] | [registrar] [hasRegistryRole] [Registrar] | [issuer] [hasRegistryRole] [Holder] | [investor1] [hasRegistryRole] [Holder] |
| Credential issuer | Digital Asset | Provider | Provider | Provider |
