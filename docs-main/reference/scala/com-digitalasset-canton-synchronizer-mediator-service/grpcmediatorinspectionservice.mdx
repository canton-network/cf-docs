---
title: "GrpcMediatorInspectionService"
description: "The mediator inspection service delivers a stream of finalized verdicts. The verdicts are sorted by the finalization time, which additionally is capped by the watermark tracked via the provided time awaiter. In case the watermark is reached and no new verdicts are found, the stream waits to be notified by the time awaiter when new watermarks are encountered. In practice, the time awaiter follows the observed sequencing."
---
