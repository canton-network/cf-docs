# Commercial Agreement

Digital Asset provides multiple services to clients with the Utility. Some of these services (such
as the billing processing for paid credentials) incur a service fee.

To utilize these services, a commercial agreement must be established with Digital Asset.

This agreement, issued by Digital Asset, can be accepted via the user interface which contains the
service fee terms. Click on the settings icon in the top right corner and go to Commercial
Agreements. Once the agreement is accepted, a Canton Coin deposit must be locked and regularly
topped up to access paid services.

For more information about licensing and pricing, please contact Digital Asset.
