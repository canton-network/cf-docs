# Token Transfer

## Investor1 offers token transfer from Investor2

Investor1 (as the holder of BOND) offers transfer of BOND to Investor2 (another holder of BOND)

| Actor     | Utility Module   |
|-----------|------------------|
| Investor1 | REGISTRY         |

Select HOLDINGS on the left navigation. The 10,000 BOND is there.

![image](tutorials/transfer/pictures/transfer-1.png)

Click TRANSFER. A window pops up for input.

- Receiver: Investor2’s Party ID
- Amount: 2000
- Reference: <anything or empty>

![image](tutorials/transfer/pictures/transfer-2.png)

Click OFFER.

## Investor2 accepts the transfer offer and tokens are transferred

Investor2 (as the holder of BOND) accepts the transfer offers.

| Actor     | Utility Module   |
|-----------|------------------|
| Investor2 | REGISTRY         |

Select TRANSFERS on the left navigation. The transfer offer is shown.

![image](tutorials/transfer/pictures/transfer-3.png)

Click ACCEPT. A window pops up.

![image](tutorials/transfer/pictures/transfer-4.png)

Click ACCEPT. The transfer is executed.

Select HOLDINGS on the left navigation. The minted 2,000 BOND is owned by Investor2.

![image](tutorials/transfer/pictures/transfer-5.png)

Check BOND holdings for all entities

| Actor                                   | Utility Module   |

Select HOLDINGS on the left navigation.

Registrar (see holdings for all entities)

Issuer (none)

Congratulations! The transfer (asset delivery) is complete.
