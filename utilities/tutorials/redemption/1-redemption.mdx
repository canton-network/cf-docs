# Token Redemption

## Investor1 returns all holdings to Issuer

Investor1 (as the holder of BOND) offers transfer of BOND to Issuer (another holder of BOND)

| Actor     | Utility Module   |
|-----------|------------------|
| Investor1 | REGISTRY         |

Select HOLDINGS on the left navigation. The 8,000 BOND is there.

![image](tutorials/redemption/pictures/redemption-1.png)

Click TRANSFER. A window pops up for input.

- Receiver: Issuer’s Party ID
- Amount: 8000
- Reference: &lt;anything or empty&gt;

![image](tutorials/redemption/pictures/redemption-2.png)

Click OFFER.

## Investor2 returns all holdings to Issuer

Investor2 (as the holder of BOND) offers transfer of BOND to Issuer (another holder of BOND)

## Issuer accepts transfers

Issuer (as the holder of BOND) accepts the transfer offers.

| Actor   | Utility Module   |
|---------|------------------|
| Issuer  | REGISTRY         |

Select TRANSFERS on the left navigation. The transfer offer is shown.

![image](tutorials/redemption/pictures/redemption-5.png)

Click ACCEPT. A window pops up.

![image](tutorials/redemption/pictures/redemption-6.png)

Click ACCEPT. The transfer is executed.

Select HOLDINGS on the left navigation. All holdings are now owned by Issuer.

(Note: Currently the holdings are not merged, and it may change in later releases.)

![image](tutorials/redemption/pictures/redemption-7.png)

## Issuer requests burning

Issuer (as the holder of BOND) request burning of tokens

| Actor   | Utility Module   |
|---------|------------------|
| Issuer  | REGISTRY         |

Select HOLDINGS on the left navigation. Click REQUEST BURN. A window pops up for input.

![image](tutorials/redemption/pictures/redemption-8.png)

Input the following information

- Instrument: BOND
- Amount: 10000
- Registrar: Registrar’s Party ID
- Reference: &lt;anything or empty&gt;

![image](tutorials/redemption/pictures/redemption-9.png)

Click REQUEST.

## Registrar accepts burning and tokens are burned

Registrar (as the registrar of BOND) accepts the mint request

| Actor     | Utility Module   |
|-----------|------------------|
| Registrar | REGISTRY         |

Select BURNS on the left navigation. The burn request is shown.

![image](tutorials/redemption/pictures/redemption-10.png)

Click ACCEPT. The 10,000 BOND is burned.

Select HOLDINGS on the left navigation. There are no holdings in the Registrar.

![image](tutorials/redemption/pictures/redemption-11.png)

Congratulations! The redemption is complete.
