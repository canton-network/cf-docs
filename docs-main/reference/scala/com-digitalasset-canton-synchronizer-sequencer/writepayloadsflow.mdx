---
title: "WritePayloadsFlow"
description: "Extract the payloads of events and write them in batches to the payloads table. As order does not matter at this point allow writing batches concurrently up to the concurrency specified by SequencerWriterConfig.payloadWriteMaxConcurrency. Pass on the events with the payloads dropped and replaced by their payload ids."
---
