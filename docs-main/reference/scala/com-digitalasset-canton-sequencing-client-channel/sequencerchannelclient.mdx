---
title: "SequencerChannelClient"
description: "Sequencer channel client for interacting with GRPC-based sequencer channels that exchange data with other nodes bypassing the regular canton protocol and ordering-based sequencer service. In addition, the SequencerChannelClient tracks channels allowing the client to close channels when the client is closed."
---
