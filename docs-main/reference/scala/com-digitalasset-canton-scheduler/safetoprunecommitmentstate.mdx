---
title: "SafeToPruneCommitmentState"
description: "Defines which counter-participants cannot block pruning because of the states of their commitments. The modes increasingly relax the pruning conditions. These modes override the default, which is that all counter-participants block pruning when there isn't a timestamp where all counter-participants sent matching commitments, and non-matching participants are not in a no-wait configuration."
---
